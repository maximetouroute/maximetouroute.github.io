We organized six augmented reality creation workshops for the summer program at the Carrefour Numérique in the Cité des Sciences. We helped participants to film themselves and make their own virtual doubles with the _Reveality_ app. Each participant projected its very own double in location and in augmented realiy.
The content produced during these workshops was exhibited to the general public for two months in a section accessible on the app.
