# In Brief
A monumental 200 m² mural in the popular neighborhood _Zodiaque_ of [Annonay](https://www.annonay.fr/), co-created with residents.

* __**Participatory Workshops and Social and Professional Integration Project**:__ 10+ young people employed in an inclusive program
* __Resident-driven creation:__ choices of content and colors; Augmented Reality content made by the residents
* __Augmented Reality:__ filming the mural reveals audio and video stories about the residents' lives
* __Evolving project:__ with Revy, residents and local organizations can run their own workshops and add new AR content to the mural

## A Story Told Through Augmented Reality

Film the mural to unlock an intangible layer of sound testimonies and videos. Residents created six scenes that depict their stories, cultures, neighborhood life, and the city of Annonay.
