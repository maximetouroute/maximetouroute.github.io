A participatory artwork where the public is invited to draw live on walls. Massively interactive, everyone can participate simultaneously by drawing with their phone.

![Corsica_AAA_0030©MaximeTouroute.jpg](Corsica_AAA_0030©MaximeTouroute.jpg)

<ImageGrid props={props.data.mdx.frontmatter.embeddedImagesLocal} indices={[0, 1]} gridSize={2} />

By leveraging technology to amplify collaborative creation, Let’s Draw reveals our shared imagination around universal themes: How do we draw a tree? How do we draw love? How do we draw our world?
<br/>

<ImageGrid props={props.data.mdx.frontmatter.embeddedImagesLocal} indices={[2,3,4,5,6,7]} gridSize={3} />

A creation that comes alive both on the facade and within the crowd, as participants of all ages draw, react, and engage with their own creations and those of others in a convivial and collective atmosphere.
Local artists join the project and perform in live.
