_Live Maker_ is a **shared software** for the creative community that transforms smartphones into broadcasting and interaction platforms.


## Features

* **Real-time interaction retrieval**: photos, drawings, sounds, joystick, accelerometer.
* **Direct content broadcasting on smartphones**: video, audio, text, vibrator, flash.
* **No app download required**: accessible via a simple web page through a link or QR code.
* **Supports up to 10,000 simultaneous users**: whether onsite or spread across the globe.
* **Works on 4G, Wi-Fi, or offline**: adaptable to any context.
* **Live control**: back-office software for managing interactions and OSC compatibility.
