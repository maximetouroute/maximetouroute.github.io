Passionate by cinema, photography and digital arts, I work on projects where creativity and technology meet.

Being a full-fledged engineer, I'm not afraid to work with state of the art technologies to unlock new creative possibilities.

### — What I do

* Join artistic teams as a developer, technician, videographer
* Create and exhibit digital artworks
* Build and share software with cultural institutions and artists


### — My skilsl

* Software development (wab, AI, 3D, native...)
* Art Direction, Production
* Project management, team management
* Entrepreneurship
* Film making, from shooting to post-production
