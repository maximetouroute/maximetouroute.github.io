**Le Phare** (The Lighthouse) is an interactive installation with a 360° projection system. Children are invited to use a boat wheel to control the projection.

_Le Phare invites us to look at the horizon beyond city walls. It projects intimate figures talking about the sacred and forgotten links between humans and nature. Meanwhile, on the walls, sea level is rising..._
