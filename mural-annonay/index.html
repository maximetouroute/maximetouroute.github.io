Annonay Mural

Augmented Street Art

A 200mÂ² participative mural blending Paint and Digital Art


In Brief

A monumental 200 mÂ² mural in the popular neighborhood Zodiaque of Annonay, co-created with residents.

  • Participatory Workshops and Social and Professional Integration Project: 10+ young people employed in an inclusive program
  • Resident-driven creation: choices of content and colors; Augmented Reality content made by the residents
  • Augmented Reality: filming the mural reveals audio and video stories about the residents' lives
  • Evolving project: with Revy, residents and local organizations can run their own workshops and add new AR content to the mural

A Story Told Through Augmented Reality

Film the mural to unlock an intangible layer of sound testimonies and videos. Residents created six scenes that depict their stories, cultures, neighborhood life, and the city of Annonay.



Participative from planning to completion

With collective workshops for conception and painting jobs for production, providing work experience for over 10 young people.

Opening Event

A grand opening bringing together locals and artists with performances and convivial moments.

Powered by Revy

The Augmented Reality dimension was made possible by the intuitive creation tools in Revy, enabling workshops where participants fully engage in the creation process and express themselves.
